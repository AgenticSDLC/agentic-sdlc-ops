## Key Message (the north star)

> **Agentic SDLC Ops is governance for agentic delivery without platform lock-in.**
>
> It's a portable, lightweight execution contract that teams adopt in minutes—not weeks—and adapt to their own stack and constraints. No centralized platform. No forced migration. Just clear rules about when agents can work, how humans can steer, and what evidence is required before merge.

Use this phrase to anchor all communication.
